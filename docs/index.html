How to use:

            
Enter the Sudoku board and click the play button to solve the puzzle.

            
            
        

        

            
You can enter the Sudoku board in multiple ways:

            
    
                
  • Upload an image: take a picture or choose an image from your device.
    • 
                
  • Enter the board as a text line in the input field.
    • 
                
  • Edit the board by clicking on the interactive cells.
    • 
            

            
            
        

        

        
If you upload an image, crop the Sudoku board and confirm for best results.

            
You can also edit the board by clicking on the cells or using the input field.
